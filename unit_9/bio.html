Bill Gibson, Guitarist

Timeless music for public and private events

Biography

Bill began playing professionally while a music student at San Jose State University, where he earned a Bachelor of Music in Classical Guitar Performance.

Starting out playing in coffee shops and restaurants, Bill has since played at hundreds of private and public events at many of the premier venues in northern California, inlcuding the San Francisco Maritime Museum, the Stanford University Memorial Church, and the Lodge at Pebble Beach.
